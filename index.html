Chocolate Chip Cookies

Total Time: 20-22 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter, white sugar, and brown sugar together. Add eggs and vanilla, and mix well.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add to the wet mixture.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes, until golden brown.


Brownies

Total Time: 35-40 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x9 inch baking pan.
  2. Mix melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Add cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder. Stir until well combined.
  4. Pour batter into the pan and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.


Apple Crisp

Total Time: 45-50 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups peeled and sliced apples
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1/4 cup butter (softened)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Toss apples with sugar and cinnamon and place in a baking dish.
  3. In a separate bowl, mix flour, brown sugar, oats, and butter until crumbly.
  4. Sprinkle the crumb mixture over the apples.
  5. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown.
